    • What we do at faelabs

    Modern day science has restricted its focus to understanding, and observing the natural physical world. For many years modern science has failed to explore the supernatural phenomena that is so clearly evident in the world.

    At Faelabs Our team of passionate young biologists have pushed the boundaries of what we have accepted science capable of, creating the first, successful fairy.

    Fantasy creature, no more. Using simple cloning and cell fusion procedures on Psilocybe azurescens a species of mushroom containing the active compounds of psilocybin, and psilocin, our team created three successful trials of living fairies.
